star Founder & CEO

Ousman Jarju

Ousman Jarju is the founder and visionary behind Janta Media & Communications Services. With over 20 years of experience in media production, strategic communications, and community engagement, Ousman has built a career dedicated to amplifying the voices of the African Diaspora and immigrant communities.

A skilled filmmaker, media strategist, and cultural bridge-builder, Ousman brings deep expertise in audiovisual storytelling, brand development, and multicultural communications. His work spans documentaries, outreach campaigns, language interpretation services, and cultural competency training for government and private sector partners.

Based in Marysville, Washington, Ousman serves Snohomish County, North King County, and communities nationwide — leading JMCS with a commitment to culturally-sensitive, impactful communication.

Pa Ousman Joof

Pa Ousman Joof is the co-founder and strategic partnerships lead at Janta Media & Communications Services. With extensive experience in community development, public engagement, and cross-cultural communications, Pa Ousman plays a critical role in building the relationships that power JMCS's impact.

Pa Ousman's deep roots in the African Diaspora community and his expertise in stakeholder engagement make him an invaluable bridge between organizations and the communities they serve. He leads JMCS's interpretation services and cultural competency training programs.
