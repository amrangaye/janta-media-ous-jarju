Meet the Team

The passionate professionals behind JMCS — united by a shared commitment to empowering the African Diaspora through media and communication.

Leadership

Executive Team

Ousman Jarju

Founder & Director

Ousman Jarju is a multifaceted professional, community leader, and the driving force behind Janta Media & Communications Services. He manages the day-to-day business operations and strategic vision of JMCS.

With extensive expertise spanning community engagement, audio-visual production, event organization, and African language translation, Ousman brings a unique blend of skills that positions JMCS at the forefront of culturally sensitive media services.

His leadership is informed by deep roots in the African Diaspora community and a genuine passion for bridge-building between communities and institutions.

Community Leader Media Professional Language Expert Cultural Liaison

Pa Ousman Joof

Executive Leader & Advisor

Pa Ousman Joof brings extensive strategic insight and executive guidance to Janta Media & Communications Services, ensuring operational excellence across all community initiatives.

Dedicated to empowering the African Diaspora, he works closely with the founding team to build sustainable partnerships and expand the reach of vital communication services.

His vision and commitment to the community continually elevate JMCS's ability to serve as a pivotal bridge for information and development.
